WPP Stream

Stream, the WPP (un)conference, brings together 300 industry leaders, from brands, technology companies and agencies, for three days, usually on a beach. The purpose? To learn from, and contribute to, the freshest thinking around the future of technology and communications

Upcoming Events

Stream India 2017

Hosted by Ranjan Kapur, Chairman, WPP India, Stream India is a private gathering of just 250 leaders from the technology, marketing and media industries across the Indian Subcontinent from 15-18 February

Stream SXSW 2017

Stream does SXSW! No PPTX presentations. No pitches. No panels. Instead, we bring together an eclectic, but influential group of CEOs, VCs, CMOs, CDOs to discuss and debate the future of digital.

Stream Asia 2017

Hosted by Scott Spirit, CDO, WPP and Investor Yossi Vardi, Stream Asia brings together a small number of creative, media and technology leaders from our agencies, clients and media owners for three days of discussion and debate in Thailand on 16-19 March

Stream Cannes 2017

Hosted by Martin Sorrell, CEO, WPP, Stream Cannes brings together a small group of clients, agency CEOs and technology and media leaders to join us on an island off the coast of Cannes for Stream; a lunch and afternoon of talks and discussion on 20th June
